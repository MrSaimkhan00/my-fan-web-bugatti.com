Our Legacy

Bugatti Automobiles is a world-renowned manufacturer of high-performance luxury vehicles. Founded in 1909 by Italian-born French automobile designer Ettore Bugatti, the brand is known for its cutting-edge engineering, elegant designs, and unmatched performance on the road. Bugatti's legacy continues to push the boundaries of automotive excellence with a focus on speed, luxury, and exclusivity.
